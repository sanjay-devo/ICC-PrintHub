Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our online printing service

How does online printing work?

ICC PrintHub makes online printing simple. Upload your document, select your print preferences (color, quality, binding), and we'll print and deliver to your doorstep within 24 hours. No need to visit a print shop anymore.

Can I upload PDF files?

Yes! PDF files are fully supported. We accept PDF, DOC, DOCX, JPG, PNG, PPT, and XLS formats. Simply upload your PDF file and we'll handle the professional printing.

What print options are available?

We offer color printing and black & white printing options. You can also choose paper quality, add spiral binding, select page arrangement, and specify quantity. Customize everything to your needs.

How much does printing cost?

Pricing is transparent and depends on the number of pages, color options, paper quality, and binding preferences. Visit our pricing page for detailed rates or contact us for a custom quote.

Is doorstep delivery available?

Yes! Doorstep delivery is available across India including Bhopal, Singrauli, Madhya Pradesh, and all major cities. We deliver directly to your home or office address within 24 hours.

How long does delivery take?

Standard doorstep delivery takes 24 hours from order confirmation. Express delivery options may be available for urgent printing needs. Track your order anytime through our tracking system.

Do you offer spiral binding?

Yes, professional spiral binding is available. Perfect for theses, projects, portfolios, and business reports. Select spiral binding when placing your order and we'll handle everything.

Are color prints available?

Absolutely! We offer high-quality color printing for brochures, flyers, photos, and marketing materials. Choose color printing when customizing your print settings.

Which file formats are supported?

We support: PDF, DOC, DOCX, JPG, PNG, PPT, and XLS. If your file is in another format, please contact us. Most common document and image formats are accepted.

Is my uploaded file secure?

Yes! We use 100% secure file uploads with industry-standard encryption. Your documents are protected and never shared with third parties. Your privacy is our priority.
