Delete Your PrintHub Account

Uploaded documents and print files are automatically deleted after the order is completed and delivered. Certain transaction and order records may be retained for legal, tax, accounting, or fraud prevention purposes. PrintHub by India Cyber Cafe allows users to request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

To request account deletion, please email us from your registered mobile number or email account.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to icc@indiacybercafe.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Delete My PrintHub Account.
  3. Include your registered phone number.
  4. Our team will verify your request and process it.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User profile information
  • Phone number and account details
  • Uploaded documents and files
  • Saved addresses
  • Notification preferences

Data That May Be Retained

  • Order history required for legal, tax, or accounting purposes
  • Transaction records required by applicable laws

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 7 business days.

For assistance, contact: icc@indiacybercafe.com
